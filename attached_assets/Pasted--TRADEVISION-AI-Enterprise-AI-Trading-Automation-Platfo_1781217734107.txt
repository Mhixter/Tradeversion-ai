# TRADEVISION AI

## Enterprise AI Trading Automation Platform

### Vision

Build the world's leading AI-powered trading automation platform that connects directly to MT4 and MT5 broker accounts, allowing traders, teams, strategy creators, and institutions to automate trading through professional AI bots, custom strategies, copy trading, and marketplace subscriptions.

The platform NEVER holds customer funds.

All funds remain with the user's broker.

The platform only:

* Reads broker data
* Executes trades
* Manages risk
* Provides analytics
* Hosts AI bots
* Hosts strategy marketplace
* Provides copy trading
